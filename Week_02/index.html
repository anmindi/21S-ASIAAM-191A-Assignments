About Me

Hi there! My name is Andi, I use she/her pronouns, and I am a graduate student at UCLA.

I am in my 1st year of my Master of Public Health program and 2nd year of my MA program for Asian American Studies.

I am passionate about healthy equity and immigration justice.

My current class projects include addressing food insecurty through school-based interventions and increasing health access among elderly Asian immigrants.

As a critical digital map maker, my goals are to...

  1. democratize data
  2. emphasize traditional knowledges
  3. question social constructs
